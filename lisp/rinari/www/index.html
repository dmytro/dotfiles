Rinari

Rinari Is Not A Ruby IDE.

Well, ok it kind of is. Rinari is a set of Emacs Lisp modes that is aimed towards making Emacs into a top-notch Ruby and Rails development environment.

Right now it's mostly in the planning and early hacking stage. We're gathering ideas and have some initial code, but it's not quite ready for mass consumtion. There is the beginning of a tutorial aimed towards explaining how it works from a high level. If you want to help or you've got ideas about functionality you wish Emacs had, please contact the developers via Rubyforge; or #ruby-lang on freenode. We are always open to new ideas.
